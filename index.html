What is it?

SpeechTranscribe turns your spoken words into text instantly. Our speech recognition technology processes and displays the corresponding text in near real time.

What is it for?

Whether in business meetings, classes, interviews or in any situation where communication is essential, SpeechTranscribe stands out for its efficiency and practicality.

Very useful in moments when it would be difficult to use a keyboard, or pencil and paper; SpeechTranscribe allows you to speed up and save your ideas when you practice your daily walk, while stuck in traffic.
